ADVOCATE, THEN ACT! (ATA)

The official website of a youth-led project to help Butuan's Barangay Masao.










OUR SERVICES


Cleanup Drives

We aim to reduce the number of trash located on the coasts of Butuan's Barangay Masao.

Workshops and Trainings

We aim to educate the barangay population on climate change and proper waste segregation through workshops and trainings.

Collaborations with Researches

We aim to use our platform to promote scientific research and the pursuit for discovery.
